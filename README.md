## ✨ What is Nova?

Nova is an offline, voice-driven AI system designed to operate in the real world — not just on screens.

It listens, responds, remembers, and moves — all locally, without relying on distant servers.

No latency.  
No dependency.  
No abstraction.  

**Just presence.**

---

## ⚡ Core Capabilities

- 🎙️ **Natural Voice Interaction**  
  Speak to Nova as you would to a person.

- 🧠 **Local AI Processing (Offline-First)**  
  Nova runs on-device — no cloud required.

- 🧍 **Physical Presence**  
  Motion, gesture, and timing generated in real time.

- 🔁 **Persistent Memory**  
  Context builds over time — interactions evolve.

- 🔒 **Privacy by Design**  
  Your data stays with you.

---

## 🌍 Why Nova Exists

Most AI systems live in the cloud.

Nova was built to live **with you**.

It doesn’t wait on servers.  
It doesn’t simulate presence.  

It *is* presence.
